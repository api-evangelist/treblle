AI governance for APIs covers the technical practices needed to ensure AI-powered API behavior is traceable, documented, compliant, and consistently enforced across organizations. The article argues engineering teams must implement versioned OpenAPI specs, audit trails, automated governance checks, and readiness scoring systems to prepare for the AI API era.
